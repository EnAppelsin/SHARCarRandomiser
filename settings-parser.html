--- title: Settings Decoder layout: page ---

On the title screen of the Randomiser v2.0 and later versions the settings are compactly printed as hexadecimal numbers, for purposes such as comparing settings with others (and for developers). This simple page will decode the numbers into the appropriate settings.

Note: Random stat limits & debug level are not included in the settings
